About Me

I am a senior data science student originally from a small town in Peru, currently completing my undergraduate degree abroad. My academic journey has driven me to research how machine learning and data mining transform our world, and I am actively investigating these technologies to understand their full potential.

Living and studying far from home has been a profound learning experience. It has forced me to master deep self-organization, resilience, and adaptability while managing complex academic responsibilities.
Every challenge faced abroad serves as motivation to sharpen my technical capabilities.

My ultimate goal is to bring this advanced technical expertise back home to help develop my country. I am passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge computational methods and real-world impact, ensuring data-driven solutions benefit society.
